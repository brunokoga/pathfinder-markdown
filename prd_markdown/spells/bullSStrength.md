 **Bull's Strength**

**School** transmutation; **Level** cleric 2, druid 2, paladin 2, sorcerer/wizard 2

**Casting Time** 1 standard action

**Components** V, S, M/DF (a few hairs, or a pinch of dung, from a bull)

**Range** touch

**Target** creature touched

**Duration** 1 min./level

**Saving Throw** [Will](../combat.html#_will) negates (harmless); **[Spell Resistance](../glossary.html#_spell-resistance)** yes (harmless)

The subject becomes stronger. The spell grants a +4 enhancement bonus to [Strength](../gettingStarted.html#_strength), adding the usual benefits to melee attack rolls, melee damage rolls, and other uses of the [Strength](../gettingStarted.html#_strength) modifier.

**Bull's Strength, Mass**

**School** transmutation; **Level** cleric 6, druid 6, sorcerer/wizard 6

**Range** close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)

**Targets** one creature/level, no two of which can be more than 30 ft. apart

This spell functions like _bull's strength_, except that it affects multiple creatures.
