 **Persistent Image**

**School** illusion (figment); **Level** bard 5, sorcerer/wizard 5

**Duration** 1 min./level (D)

This spell functions like _ [silent image](silentImage.html#_silent-image), _except that the figment includes visual, auditory, olfactory, and thermal components, and the figment follows a script determined by you. The figment follows that script without your having to concentrate on it. The illusion can include intelligible speech if you wish.
